The 2026 Indian Premier League (IPL) took place from March 28 to May 31, 2026. 
Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) won the 2026 IPL title by defeating the Gujarat Titans (GT) by 5 wickets in the final at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. 
Virat Kohli of Royal Challengers Bengaluru scored 75 not out off 42 balls in the final and won Player of the Match.
Vaibhav Sooryavanshi of the Rajasthan Royals won the Orange Cap for the most runs (776) and was named Player of the Tournament.
Kagiso Rabada of the Gujarat Titans won the Purple Cap for taking the most wickets (29) in the season.
